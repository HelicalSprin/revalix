Speciality 01
Orthopedic
Our orthopedic team restores movement and strength after injury, surgery or degenerative conditions using manual therapy, targeted exercise and the latest technology.
  • ACL & ligament reconstruction
  • Joint replacement rehab
  • Rotator cuff & shoulder
  • Fracture & bone healing
  • Tendinopathy & bursitis

Speciality 02
Neurology
Neurological physiotherapy retrains the brain and nervous system. We work intensively with patients to regain movement, balance and independence through neuroplasticity-driven rehab.
  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Peripheral nerve injury
  • Vestibular & balance disorders

Speciality 03
Cardiorespiratory
Specialised cardiac and pulmonary rehab helping patients recover from heart conditions, surgery and respiratory diseases — safely rebuilding fitness and quality of life.
  • Post-cardiac surgery rehab
  • COPD & asthma management
  • Breathlessness & fatigue
  • Long COVID recovery
  • Cardiac fitness programmes

Elite Sports

Pediatric Rehab
